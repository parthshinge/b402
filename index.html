Frequently Asked Questions

Why keep payments under our own domain?

Maintaining your own domain for payments preserves your brand identity and user trust. It eliminates the need to redirect users to third-party payment processors, creating a seamless experience.

Do clients or servers manage private keys?

Private keys are securely managed by Privy's infrastructure. Neither clients nor servers have direct access to private keys, ensuring maximum security while maintaining ease of use.

Can users bring their own wallet?

Yes! While we provide seamless wallet creation through Privy, we also support integration with external wallets for users who prefer to manage their own keys.

Do agents need UI to pay?

No. Our system is designed to work seamlessly with autonomous agents. Payments can be handled programmatically without any user interface required.

How are transactions monitored?

All transactions are recorded on the BNB Chain and can be monitored in real-time through our dashboard, providing complete transparency and auditability.

What about compliance and KYC?

We provide tools to help with compliance, including optional KYC integration. However, the level of KYC required is up to each individual implementation.

How It Works

1

Request

A buyer (agent) requests a resource from a b402-enabled service.

2

Payment Required

Service responds with 402 Payment Required and b402-formatted payment instructions.

3

Payment

Buyer sends payment payload to the b402 facilitator for settlement.

4

Verification

Server verifies the transaction through /verify and /settle endpoints.

5

Access Granted

Once verified, the server releases the requested resource.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is b402?

b402 is an open payment protocol standard on BNB Chain that enables seamless agent-to-agent payments with native crypto primitives.

How does b402 handle identity?

b402 uses ERC-8004 for verified agent transactions, ensuring secure and authenticated interactions.

What are the transaction fees?

Transaction fees are minimal, leveraging BNB Chain's low-cost infrastructure while maintaining security and speed.

Is b402 compatible with other blockchains?

While initially deployed on BNB Chain, b402 is designed with EVM compatibility in mind for future expansion.
